Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of technology enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for sharing cutting-edge innovations. What started as a small gathering of 200 attendees has grown into one of the premier tech conferences worldwide, with over 10,000 participants annually.

Over the years, we've hosted conferences in 15 different countries and featured groundbreaking presentations that have shaped the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to bridge the gap between technology innovators and the global community. We aim to:

  • Foster innovation through knowledge sharing
  • Create networking opportunities for tech professionals
  • Promote diversity and inclusion in the tech industry
  • Showcase emerging technologies that can solve real-world problems
