Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small regional meetup for tech enthusiasts and has grown into an international event attended by thousands of professionals, innovators, and students. Each year, the conference expands its reach, offering cutting-edge insights into technology and innovation.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, support diversity in tech, and provide a platform where the future of technology is explored and shaped. We aim to connect industry leaders, educators, and aspiring tech professionals through meaningful engagement.

Notable Past Speakers

AI Ethics Researcher and Professor at Stanford University, Dr. Liang spoke about responsible AI development and societal impacts of machine learning.

Jason McNeil

Founder of NeoTech Labs, Jason discussed the rise of quantum computing and how it's poised to revolutionize modern cryptography.
